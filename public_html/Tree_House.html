Overview

Escape to ResTer Tree Houses' enchanted realm, where childhood fantasies come true beneath the canopy of trees. Our tree homes provide a quirky and distinctive accommodation experience that immerses you in a fantastical and beautiful natural setting. These hidden havens, which are elevated above the surface, offer a private hideaway for adventure and leisure. Experience the sights, sounds, and sweeping vistas of the forest as they change with the seasons. Relax in a comfortable and exquisitely made tree home while you unplug from the mundane and embrace the exceptional. Whether you're looking for a family vacation or a romantic break, our tree homes provide a fantastical haven that ensures priceless memories.

Our Tariff

At ResTer Hotels, we offer a range of accommodation options to suit your preferences and needs. Our tariff varies based on the type of stay you choose and the amenities provided.

Standard Tree House: $120 - $200 per night
Deluxe Tree House: $180 - $300 per night
Premium Tree House: $250 - $400 per night

In and Around

Staying in ResTer's Tree Houses, located above the ground among the treetops, will heighten your senses. Enjoy a beautiful morning vista and the sound of rustling leaves as you awaken. Take use of the options for canopy hikes, zip line, and animal sightings to fully immerse yourself in the natural environment. Immerse yourself in the forest's marvels as you discover its mysteries. Your stay in a tree house guarantees a unique connection to nature thanks to the comforts of our distinctive lodgings and the allure of the surrounding forest.
